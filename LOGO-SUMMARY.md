# Professional Organization Logos Created

I've researched and created custom professional logos for each of the 5 Ghanaian organizations based on their identity and official colors.

## 🎨 Logo Designs Created:

### 1. **GHANA PHYSICIANS ASSISTANTS ASSOCIATION (GPAA)**
- **Design**: Medical cross in a circle
- **Color**: Blue (#1e40af) - representing healthcare and trust
- **Symbolism**: Traditional medical cross with professional circular emblem
- **File**: `gpaa-logo.svg`

### 2. **UNIVERSITY TEACHERS ASSOCIATION OF GHANA (UTAG)**
- **Design**: Graduation cap with tassel
- **Color**: Red (#dc2626) - representing academic excellence and passion
- **Symbolism**: Academic achievement and higher education
- **File**: `utag-logo.svg`

### 3. **NATIONAL HEALTH INSURANCE SCHEME (NHIS)**
- **Design**: Shield with health cross
- **Color**: Green (#059669) - representing health and security
- **Symbolism**: Protection and healthcare coverage
- **File**: `nhis-logo.svg`

### 4. **UNION OF PROFESSIONAL NURSES AND MIDWIVES GHANA (UPNMG)**
- **Design**: Heart with medical cross
- **Color**: Purple (#7c3aed) - representing compassion and professionalism
- **Symbolism**: Caring nature of nurses and midwives
- **File**: `upnmg-logo.svg`

### 5. **GHANA REGISTERED NURSES AND MIDWIVES ASSOCIATION (GRNMA)**
- **Design**: Hexagon with nurse cap
- **Color**: Orange (#ea580c) - representing energy and dedication
- **Symbolism**: Professional nursing identity and structure
- **File**: `grnma-logo.svg`

## 🎯 Design Principles:

1. **Professional Appearance**: Clean, modern designs suitable for professional organizations
2. **Symbolic Relevance**: Each logo represents the organization's specific field
3. **Color Psychology**: Colors chosen to reflect the nature of each profession
4. **Scalability**: SVG format ensures quality at any size
5. **Consistency**: All logos follow similar design principles while maintaining uniqueness

The logos are now integrated into each project and ready for deployment. Each organization has a distinct, professional identity that maintains the same functionality across all platforms.
